<h2>Overview</h2>
<p> Let me show you how important a<a href="https://www.magezon.com/magento-2-ajax-cart-pro.html"> Magento 2 Ajax Cart </a> is! E-commerce today is not something too new for sellers. Hence, your online store has to compete with others having similar products. To make your site outstanding, the key factor is to make it <strong>easier</strong> and <strong>faster </strong> in the shopping process. Imagine about a circumstance. Your customers are adding a product to cart from your page. The thing is they always have to wait for redirecting and loading page cause the native Magento 2 automatically transfers them to the product page.  To continue shopping they have to come back to the previous or next catalog page. And how do they feel? Of course, it's not a pleasant experience. They might quit your site someday, especially when they find others having more convenient buying button.</p>
<p>Knowing this pain point deeply,  we provide our Magento 2 Ajax Cart which lets your customers shop with less interruption. Thus, it <strong>eliminates exit rates</strong>, enhance your sales by creating a positive shopping experience.</p>
<p>We get the mission of making your customers' shopping process <strong>smooth, comfortable</strong> and<strong> fast</strong>. Thus, our Magento 2 Ajax Add to Cart <strong>equips your site with the add-to-cart pop-up</strong>. As a result, your customers can specify required product options and <strong>add to carts directly</strong>. They can add items even <strong> from the product listing, catalog pages</strong> or anywhere having the add-to-cart button.</p>
<h2>Business Vallues</h2>
<h3>Saving time for customers</h3>
<p>Magento Ajax Add to Cart helps speed up the shopping process thanks to the <strong>elimination of extra steps. No more redirecting product page, no more reloading </strong> again and again. Which Magento 2 Ajax Cart, just stay right in your product catalog adding items to cart directly. Furthermore, your customers can add the items anywhere from your site as long as there is an add to cart button. No need to looking for the buying button like other traditional stores.</p>
<h3>Enhance customers experiences and loyalty</h3>
<p>Your customers will be in love with shopping from your site and retain loyalty. It's not only because of<strong> quick order progress</strong> but also the <strong>quick canceling orders</strong> without reloading. Magento 2 Ajax Cart let they cut down time wasting on product detail page editing the variations of the product anymore. Now, it’s easier than ever now to edit or erase a wrong order you've made. Just by clicking on the cart icon on the site's top-right corner, such a smooth and comfortable shopping experience!</p>
<h3>Boost your sales</h3>
<p>One more amazing feature of Magento Ajax Cart is that it allows displaying (or Not) related products whether they are<strong> up-sells or cross-sells</strong> seamlessly on the Ajax cart pop-up right below selected items. It is absolutely an effective way to raise your sales as your customers can add the whole collection just in one place.</p>

  </b></h3>
 <p>

 </p>
